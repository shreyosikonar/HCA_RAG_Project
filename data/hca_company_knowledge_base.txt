# Hari Chand Anand & Co. (HCA) - Company Knowledge Base

Hari Chand Anand & Co. (HCA) was established in the year 1910 and is one of the largest sales and service corporations of industrial sewing machines and accessories in the Indian subcontinent. The founder of the company is Sri Hari Chand Anand

The founder, Shri Hari Chand Anand, was trained at Singer and eventually became the first Asian General Manager of Singer Machines. His vision laid the foundation for a company that would become a trusted name in the industrial sewing industry.

The legacy of the founder was carried forward by Mr. D.C. Anand, who received specialized training at Adler, Germany, from 1933 to 1939. He managed the business from Lahore before the partition of India and later continued operations from Delhi after 1947.

Today, Hari Chand Anand & Co. is led by Mr. Anil Anand, an Economics graduate and the third generation in the family business. He has traveled extensively across the world to learn about the latest technologies, production methods, automation solutions, and best practices in industrial manufacturing. He is also actively preparing the next generation to continue the company's legacy.

With more than a century of experience, HCA has developed deep expertise in solving manufacturing and garmenting challenges through advanced sewing technology, automation, and technical consultation. The company is supported by a nationwide sales and service network along with a team of highly trained and experienced professionals.

HCA operates through more than 10 offices across India and maintains an extensive network of dealers and resellers. This allows the company to provide quick service, technical support, and product availability to customers throughout the country.

The company serves a wide range of industries, including:

* Garment manufacturing
* Leather garments
* Fashion accessories
* Upholstery
* Bedding and mattresses
* Footwear and shoe manufacturing
* Furniture production
* Parachute manufacturing
* Trampoline manufacturing
* Tent manufacturing
* Surgical and medical equipment
* Bag manufacturing
* Automobile seat cover manufacturing
* Defense and ordnance-related industries

Hari Chand Anand & Co. is widely recognized as a complete solution provider for the sewing industry rather than merely an equipment supplier. The company focuses on understanding customer requirements and delivering the most suitable technology solutions to improve productivity, efficiency, quality, and automation.

The organization provides a variety of products and services, including industrial sewing machines, sewing machine accessories, automation solutions, production enhancement systems, technical consultation, machine installation, operator training, and maintenance support.

The company's vision is reflected in its slogan:

"Making Automation Affordable"

HCA is committed to bringing the latest and most advanced technologies to manufacturers while ensuring affordability and ease of adoption for businesses of all sizes.

The company also provides comprehensive after-sales support services, including:

* Machine servicing
* Spare parts support
* Preventive maintenance
* Technical troubleshooting
* Technology consultation
* Process improvement guidance
* Customer training and assistance

Customer satisfaction, innovation, reliability, and technical excellence remain the core values of the organization.

In addition to its traditional business operations, HCA is continuously exploring modern digital technologies and automation solutions. The company encourages innovation, process optimization, and the adoption of advanced technologies to improve industrial productivity and manufacturing efficiency.

The organization supports learning and professional development through practical projects, real-world problem-solving, technical training, and industry exposure. Interns and professionals working with HCA gain opportunities to understand industrial automation, emerging technologies, customer requirements, and business operations.

Through its strong technical expertise, extensive service network, commitment to innovation, and over 100 years of industry experience, Hari Chand Anand & Co. has established itself as one of India's most trusted partners for industrial sewing, automation, and manufacturing technology solutions.
